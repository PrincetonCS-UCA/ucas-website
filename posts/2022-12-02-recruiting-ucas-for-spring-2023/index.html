        Recruiting UCAs for Spring 2023

        December 2, 2022

        The TigerUHR campaign for Spring 2023 will open at the end of January 2023, when everyone’s term schedules is clearer.

        If you want to be kept in the loop, don’t hesitate to fill-in this notice of interest. This will let us know to reach out to you directly when applications for Spring 2023 open up.

        Please note that it is important to submit this notice of interest even if you have worked us, as we may not automatically contact previously hired UCAs (for no particular reason other that we don’t want to spam people).
